About

A brief history of GroundUp

  • March 2020

    Our Humble Beginnings

    The founders of Ground Up started by playing video games online and discussing how they would allow each of their uniqpue personalities to get together and start something great.

  • June 2020

    An Agency is Born

    With two of the founders being unemployed due to the global Corona virus outbreak, the founding partners of Ground Up decided to open up our agency this time around.

  • August 2020

    Transition to Full Service

    After having a few rough patches, the team improved and gained more clients. We at GroundUp have managed to get both small and big clients and we have managed to retain and provide satifactory results for all our clients.

  • September 2022

    Phase Two Expansion

    Due to our reputation and word on the streets, more clients look foward to working with us. We currently building and were bigger than ever !
